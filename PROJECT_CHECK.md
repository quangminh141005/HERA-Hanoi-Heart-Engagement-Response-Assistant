Intelligent AI Customer Care Assistant for Hanoi Heart Hospital
A. Background
Hanoi Heart Hospital is a Grade I cardiovascular specialty hospital and one of
Vietnam's leading referral centers for cardiac care. The hospital serves
approximately 2,500–3,000 outpatients per day, creating a significant
demand for information from patients and their families.
The most common inquiries include:
● Appointment booking
● Doctors' schedules
● Medical examination and treatment procedures
● Health insurance (BHYT) benefits
● Service pricing
● Hospital admission procedures
● Follow-up appointment guidance
● Information about the hospital's specialized medical services
Currently, these inquiries are handled through the customer service hotline, the
hospital website, social media channels, and reception staff. The large volume of
repetitive questions places considerable pressure on customer service
personnel, resulting in delayed responses and inconsistent patient experiences.

B. Challenge
Develop an AI Customer Care Assistant that can be directly integrated into the
Hanoi Heart Hospital website to assist patients and their families in accessing
hospital information and answering frequently asked questions.
The solution should, at a minimum, satisfy the following requirements:
1. Knowledge-based Question Answering
Accurately answer questions related to:
● Appointment booking
● Medical examination and treatment procedures

● Health insurance (BHYT) benefits
● Medical service pricing
● Hospital working hours
● Doctors and medical departments
● Other official hospital information
2. Hospital System Integration
Support integration with the hospital's APIs or information systems to:
● Retrieve appointment schedules
● Redirect users to appointment booking services (Website, Zalo Mini App,
or Customer Service Hotline)
● Retrieve service information when available
3. Conversational Experience
● Support text-based conversations
● Bonus for supporting Vietnamese speech recognition (ASR) and
speech synthesis (TTS)
4. Trustworthy AI Responses
All responses must be grounded in the hospital's official knowledge base.
The AI must not hallucinate or fabricate information. If sufficient information
is unavailable, the assistant should clearly state this and direct users to the
appropriate hospital support channels.
5. Emergency Handling
When detecting symptoms that may indicate a medical emergency (e.g., severe
chest pain, shortness of breath, fainting), the AI must not provide treatment
advice.
Instead, it should immediately instruct users to seek emergency medical care or
visit the hospital's Emergency Department according to the hospital's official
procedures.
6. Deployment Readiness

The solution should be deployable on the hospital's infrastructure and comply
with applicable requirements for:
● Data privacy
● Information security
● Healthcare data protection regulations
